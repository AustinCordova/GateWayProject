To-Do Page

Helicopter Glacier Dogsledding-Seward

This Alaskan experience is targeting all ages. One of the many advantures in Alaska is Dogsledding. This $519 experience gets you a helicopter ride over snow-capped peaks befoer enjoying your Dogsledding extravaganza.When landing you get to enjoy the company of all the little piuppies, then you slide around on a sled led by dogs!. This activity is located at 2100 Airport Rd, Seward, AK 99664, USA.



Arctic Circle and Northern Lights Tour

This activity is targeting all ages. In this site seeing advanture you get to spend 14-hours of your day to walk along side one of the worlds secluded roads from Fairbanks to the Arctic Circle. On this adventure you will be led by a guid who loves Alaska just as mush as you do. You will be going up the Daltan Highway, than passing by the Alaska Trans Pipeline, later crossing the Yukan River, finally you reach the edge of the Arctic Circle. On your drive back you get to watch the Northern Lights and eat lots of snacks and sandwhiches folowed by drinking warm drinks (Hotel Transport is Included).



Northern Lights Viewing at Murphy Dome

This activity is targeting all ages. In this sightseeing adventure you get to view the Northern Lights in one of the best spots in Alaska... Murphy Dome. The elevation is 2,930 feet, it is a United States Air Force General Surveillance Radar station. Snacks, drinks and hotel transportation is all included in this activity.



Fairbanks Snowmobile Adventure from North Pole 1 Hour

This activity is targeting all ages. In this breathtaking activity you particapate in a Snowmobile tour in Fairbanks. You will be led by a guid for a total of 60 minutes, going through a true winter wonderland. You will be checking out the rivers, lakes and mountains in the North Pole vicinity. The North Pole is just the bginning. After meeting your guid there you will recieve a Snowmobile safety briefing and lesson, before heading out into the cold and beginning your tour around the famous North Pole.
