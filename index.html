Welcome to Alaska

Alaska is in the farthest northwest part of the United States West Coast. Alaska became a state in January 3rd 1959, before its statehood it was an organized incorporated territory of the United States of America. This territory was at one point the Department of Alaska 1868-1884, and the District of Alaska, 1884-1912.

About Alaska

Alaska... located on the West Coast, just across the Bering Strait from Asia. This State (fouded on the 3rd of January 1959) is most famously known for The Northern Lights. Colorful lights that are shown in the sky for one purpose, for everyone in its proximity to enjoy. The Northern Lights is caused by charged particles from the sun. The suns light hits atoms from out atmosphere causing the electrons in the atoms to move to a higher energy state. Pretty cool right? Now enough about the Aurora, lets take a look at the History. Alaska goes way back to the Upper Paleolithic period (around 14,000 BC). The contruction of military bases helped with the population increase of some of thew Alaskan cities.This started after groups crossed the Bering land bridge what is now called Western Alaska.
